Covid-19 Map

About

The purpose of this game is to eudcate users on the severity of the Covid-19 Virus throughout the world. By providing a fun game, users will be able to have fun and still learn more about the statistics of Covid-19. The implemented point system will also provide users with more benefits such as the ability to view news articles after reaching a certain amount.
